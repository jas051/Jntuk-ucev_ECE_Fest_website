FILMATO


This time ECENTRA2K17 is back again, with the most rendezvous and one of the most awaited event ‘SHORTFILM CONTEST’. Through this event we hope to facilitate creative filmmakers to show their artwork, encourage talented and passionate filmmakers to express their voice, and to provide positive life through healthy laugh and meditative encouragement.

Register

List of topics

Short films with a very good concept will be encouraged.

Guidelines to Candidates

1. Each team can occupy maximum 2 participants

2. Short films which are to be presented should be own creation. The Presenter should be a part of it.

3. Downloaded short films are NOT ALLOWED.

4. The short film shall not be more than 15 minutes including the beginning and end credits.

5. The short films may be in Telugu, Hindi, English languages.

6. Candidates should register through online.

7. Judge's decision shall be final and binding

8. Last date for registration is 4th march ,2017.

9. Payment should be done at ECENTRA2K17.

Registration Fee

100/- for single candidate
150/- for two participants as a team

Notice

Judge's decision shall be final and binding.
It is mandatory to pay fee of Rs: 200/- at the reporting centre for claiming pass to participate in our fest.
