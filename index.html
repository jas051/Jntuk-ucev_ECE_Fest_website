ECENTRA is a National level technical symposium organized by Department of Electronics and Communications Engineering of Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University.
It’s one of the most awaited technical fest in the state. The main objective of ECENTRA is to bring all the technocrats and novices on a common platform to create,
shape and share ideas.
