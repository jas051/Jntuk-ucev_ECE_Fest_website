PAPER PRESENTATION


Presenting is not a talent. It is a skill. If you are aspiring to be in the field of research, we are providing you a path to present your work before the stalwarts. This event will give you a great experience as your innovation, knowledge and your aptitude is evaluated by an exceptional judging panel. We will support you to demonstrate your research work through this event.

Guidelines to Candidates

• Candidates can choose any topic.

• The paper should be original.

• A team should consist of maximum of two members.

• Abstract should not exceed 250 words and must be in IEEE format.

• All papers and abstracts should carry full name, college ID, contact number and email ID of the participants

• All abstracts should be mailed to submissions@ecentra.in on or before 3rd March, 2017.

• Selected abstracts will be informed through mail on or before 4th March, 2017

• The participants should carry two abstracts with them

• Selected candidates should register in our website and the registration fees should be paid at our campus

Registration Fee

200/- for single candidate as a team
300/- for two participants as a team

Notice

Judge's decision shall be final and binding.
It is mandatory to pay fee of Rs: 200/- at the reporting centre for claiming pass to participate in our fest.
